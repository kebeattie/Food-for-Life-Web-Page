Introduction

This page is an attempt to summarise the key diet advice from Tim Spectors 2022 book "Food for Life". The book draws on cutting edge research and personal insights from Professor Spector in an attempt to help us choose what to eat, and what to avoid. Subtitled "Your Guide to the New Science of Eating Well", the book goes in depth on a range of health and food related topics, including:

  • What is the microbiome?
  • Why do we love food?
  • What foods are really healthy/unhealthy?
  • How can foods effect us as unique individuals?
  • How do our food choices impact the enviroment?

Professor Spector also breaks down different types of foods and explains which we should seek/avoid.

Of course, if you're really interested in this, you should read the book for yourself! But if you don't have the time for a 400 page read, then I'll do my best to sum up the diet advice in the book!
